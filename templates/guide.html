Welcome to the Exposure Triangle App! This interactive guide will help you understand the three fundamental elements of photography exposure:

ISO

Controls your camera's sensitivity to light

Shutter Speed

Controls how long light enters your camera

Aperture

Controls how much light enters your camera

These three elements work together to create a properly exposed photograph. Changing one element often requires adjusting one or both of the others to maintain proper exposure.
